Try to guess the song in ONE second!

How To Play:

You will be played a song for one second. You then have to type in the name of the song you think it is. These songs will come from your personal library so you should know them!

The title of the song has to be exact. If your guess is incorrect, you will receive a little more time.

You will get a maximum of 6 tries and a maximum of 30 seconds of the song.

The less guesses it takes you, the more points you will receive. After 5 Songs, you will see your final score and you can play again!
